Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 01, 2026

At Charity Donation Bridge, accessible from our web platform, we prioritize the privacy of our donors.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic information required for donation processing, such as your Name, Contact Number, and Transaction details, submitted voluntarily through our forms.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Processing and tracking your donations.
  • Providing you with automated confirmation acknowledgments.
  • Maintaining internal records for administrative purposes.

3. Data Security

We use secure cloud-based systems (Google Forms & Sheets) to store your data. We do not share or sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the project administrators.
